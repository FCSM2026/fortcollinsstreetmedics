Our Collective

The Fort Collins Street Medics are a volunteer collective dedicated to providing first aid and medical support during social action and community events.

We operate on the principles of Mutual Aid, not charity. We believe everyone deserves access to care without fear or financial barrier.

Action First Aid

Treating irritants, scene safety, and rapid response in high-stress environments.

Wound Care

Hemorrhage control, infection prevention, and field bandaging techniques.

Community Care

De-escalation, hydration support, and preventative health education for all.

Community FAQ

How do I request medics? ▼

Reach out via our contact form at least 7 days before your event. Include event details, location, and expected attendance.

Need a license to join? ▼

No. We provide training for those without medical backgrounds, though professionals are also encouraged to join!
